# Initial Concept
A static landing page for the "Descomplica DEV Na Gringa" mentorship program, providing clarity on international hiring processes.

# Product Guide: Descomplica DEV Na Gringa

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** Brazilian developers seeking international careers.
- Focuses on software engineers who want to acquire clarity and predictability in international hiring processes to earn in foreign currency (USD/EUR).

## Primary Goals
- **Conversion:** Drive visitor engagement toward mentorship enrollments via Hotmart.
- **Trust Building:** Establish authority and credibility by presenting a method based on data, evidence, and auditability.

## Key Features & Sections
- **Direct Conversion:** Prominent Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons linking to the enrollment/checkout page.
- **Value Proposition:** Clear explanation of the mentorship's benefits and the "Descomplica" methodology.
- **Trust Indicators:** Integration of testimonials and proof of results to validate the mentorship's effectiveness.
